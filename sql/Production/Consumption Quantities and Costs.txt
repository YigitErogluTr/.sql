SELECT      T0.[DocDate],      YEAR(T0.[DocDate]) AS 'Belge Tarihi Yılı',     MONTH(T0.[DocDate]) AS 'Belge Tarihi Ayı',     FORMAT(T0.[DocDate], 'MMMM', 'tr-TR') AS 'Belge Tarihi Ayı',     T0.[DocEntry] AS 'Mal Çıkışı Belge No',      T0.[BaseRef] AS 'İş Emri',      T2.[ItmsGrpNam] AS 'Malzeme Grubu',     T0.[ItemCode] AS 'Kalem numarası',      T0.[Dscription] AS 'Kalem/hizmet tanımı',      T0.[WhsCode] AS 'Depo kodu',      T0.[Quantity] AS 'Tüketim Miktarı',      T0.[UomCode],     T0.[StockPrice] AS "Kalem Maliyeti",     T0.[Quantity] * T0.[StockPrice] AS 'Toplam Maliyet',     OWOR.U_FasonUretim AS 'Fason Üretim',     OUSR.USER_CODE AS 'Kullanıcı Kodu',     OUSR.U_NAME AS 'Kullanıcı Adı',     T1.[U_urungrup],     T1.U_H1_UretimBolumu,     OPRC.PrcName FROM       [dbo].[IGE1] T0 INNER JOIN      [dbo].[OITM] T1 ON T0.[ItemCode] = T1.[ItemCode] INNER JOIN      [dbo].[OITB] T2 ON T1.[ItmsGrpCod] = T2.[ItmsGrpCod] INNER JOIN      [dbo].[OWOR] OWOR ON T0.[BaseRef] = OWOR.DocNum   INNER JOIN     [dbo].[OUSR] OUSR ON OWOR.UserSign = OUSR.USERID LEFT JOIN          OPRC ON T1.U_H1_UretimBolumu = OPRC.PrcCode WHERE         T0.[ItemType] = '4'          AND T0.[BaseRef] IS NOT NULL        AND T0.[BaseRef] <> ''     AND T1.[ItmsGrpCod] NOT IN (111, 113)        AND T0.[DocDate] BETWEEN [%0] AND [%1] ORDER BY     T0.[BaseRef] ASC
