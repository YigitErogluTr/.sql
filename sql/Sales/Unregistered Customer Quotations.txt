SELECT      T0.[DocNum],      T0.[DocStatus],      T0.[Printed],      T0.[DocDate],      T0.[DocDueDate] AS 'Teklif Geçerlilik Tarihi',       T0.[CardCode],      T0.[CardName],      T0.[U_AIF_ET_BuyerName] AS 'İlgili Kişi',      T0.[U_AIF_ET_BillPhone] AS 'İlgili Kişi Telefon',      T1.[ItemCode],      T1.[Dscription],      T1.[Quantity],      T1.[Price],      T1.[U_Klise_Bedel],     T1.[Rate],      T1.[LineTotal] as"Ürün Bedeli",     T1.[LineTotal] + T1.[U_Klise_Bedel] AS 'Toplam Bedel (Ürün + Klişe)',     T1.VatPrcnt,      (T1.[LineTotal]+T1.[U_Klise_Bedel]) * (T1.[VatPrcnt]/100 ) AS 'Vergi',      ((T1.[LineTotal]+T1.[U_Klise_Bedel]) * (T1.[VatPrcnt]/100 ))+ (T1.[LineTotal] + T1.[U_Klise_Bedel] ) as "Satır Toplamı",      T3.[PymntGroup],     T0.[Comments],      T0.[U_AIF_ET_OrderNo],      T0.[NumAtCard],     T4.[TrnspName],        T2.[SlpName],     T0.[U_H1_BelgeTipi]  FROM OQUT T0   INNER JOIN QUT1 T1 ON T0.[DocEntry] = T1.[DocEntry] LEFT JOIN OSLP T2 ON T0.[SlpCode] = T2.[SlpCode] LEFT JOIN OCTG T3 ON T0.[GroupNum] = T3.[GroupNum] LEFT JOIN OSHP T4 ON T0.[TrnspCode] = T4.[TrnspCode]   WHERE T0.[CANCELED] = 'N'   AND T0.[CardCode] = 'TKLF-0001'    ORDER BY T0.[DocNum] ASC
